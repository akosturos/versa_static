Reach new markets hungry for grain

Versa is uniquely positioned to integrate sorghum grain and silage into revenue streams.

Sorghum grain opens up a number of new revenue opportunities. Sorghum grain is used worldwide as animal feed and in some markets for human consumption, notably in China. The grain is rich in starch, has comparable nutritional value to corn for animal feed and can also be used, without infrastructure modification, in corn ethanol facilities.

Versa can help our partners access these new revenue opportunities by harvesting grain as a discrete product to satisfy this growing market demand.

Versa collaborates with a global agricultural equipment manufacturer developing specialized equipment that can increase per hectare efficiencies, while lowering costs for the overall sorghum operation.
