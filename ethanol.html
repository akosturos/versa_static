Take the next step in ethanol production

“Versa’s sweet sorghum as a feedstock for ethanol production during the sugarcane off-season has technical viability inside the industry. — Fermentec, April 2015

Versa's sweet sorghum solution enables ethanol producers to fully maximize their underutilized mill capacity by using inter-crop periods and fallow lands to grow sweet sorghum, extending crushing seasons more than 60 days each season.

Our sweet sorghum solution has been validated by Fermentec to produce ethanol with yields greater than 3000 liters/hectare and in some cases greater than 5000 liters/hectare, proving sweet sorghum a viable feedstock for ethanol production, utilizing idle capital infrastructure during the off-season.

Our experienced engineers can easily integrate Versa’s sorghum solutions into existing infrastructure, requiring zero to minimal additional capital expenditures that can dramatically increase a mill's production and profitability each year. Versa's solution can make the difference between a mill making a loss or making a profit.
