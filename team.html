George Nicholas Jr.

Cheif Executive Officer

George, is an investor and developer with twenty-five years of experience in all types of real estate and mixed-use developments. His recent years have been primarily working with sugarcane, sorghum and ethanol co-generation projects assessing all phases of development from site acquisition, agronomics, overall design, debt, environmental studies, equity, financial modeling, licensing, marketing, off-take partnerships and regulatory approvals.

ANTHE GEORGE

ANTHE GEORGE

Anthe is a senior member of technical staff at the Combustion Research Facility, Sandia National Laboratories and an affiliate scientist at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory’s Joint BioEnergy Institute (JBEI). Anthe has completed scientific research for several large multi-national companies including Total, Shell and Lockheed Martin and worked at the United Nations Headquarters NYC. Anthe’s work has led to over 40 peer reviewed publications and six patents in biomass combustion, biofuels and other related areas. She has an MEng and Ph.D. in chemical engineering from Imperial College London.

ARNALDO OLINTO BASTOS NETO

Chief of Latin American Operations

Arnaldo is the founder and owner of Arbas Engenharia; with over 20 years in the sugarcane and sorghum industries. In addition Arnaldo has over 30 years of experience in all aspects of sugar and ethanol production plant design. Arnaldo has a degree in Chemical Engineering from Escola de Engenharia Mauá, and a MS in Chemical Engineering from Escola Politécnica da Universidade de São Paulo.

JORGE MAZZIOTTO

Chief Agronomist

Jorge is an agronomist with extensive expertise in crops for bioenergy applications. For the past 30 years, he has overseen sorghum plantations ranging from 1,000-20,000 ha, and has participated in sorghum adaptation studies in Mexico, Brazil, Uruguay, Panama, Dominican Republic, among others. He presently advises ALUR S.A., a Uruguayan corporation operating one of the pioneer plants worldwide producing ethanol from sweet sorghum. Jorge has advised governments and international institutions and is a professor and chair of bioenergy at the Universidad de la Republica, Uruguay.

ANTHONY KOSTUROS

Vice President

Prior to joining Versa, Anthony was at UBS Financial Wealth Management, with a team in Los Angeles focusing on international equities and commodities, and at Westar, a commercial real estate property and development firm. Anthony has a Bachelor of Science degree in finance from University of Southern California, a dual concentration in International Finance and Real Estate Finance and Development.

FERMÍN SOLANA

Director of Agronomical Implementation

Prior to Versa, Fermín joined American Express Bank where he worked for 15 years having left as Director , CFO and CAO of the Uruguay Branch. In 2001 he joined de Larrobla & Associates, and later committed to research on biofuels with focus on ethanol from sweet sorghum. Together with his partners at ACICHAN, they have created an extensive net of contacts and knowledge in the sector. Fermín graduated with a MS in Administration and Accounting at the Universidad de la República del Uruguay.
